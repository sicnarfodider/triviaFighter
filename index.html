Instructions

  1. Pick a hero (both players can have the same hero).
  2. Each player is given 10 questions to answer with varying categories and difficulty.
  3. Each difficulty has its corresponding damage value.
  4. Each hero has distinct "Power" that adds bonus damage per correct answer.
  5. A correct answer will add to your damage bank and chaining correct answers will increase your damage multiplier indicated by the 3 bars below the hero.
  6. An incorrect answer will reduce it and cancel your multipliers.
  7. To deal damage, click the "Finish Combo" button.
  8. The combo multipliers will revert back to 0 once it's used and you have to build it up again.
  9. Build up damage or be safe deal amounts of damage throughout your current turn.
